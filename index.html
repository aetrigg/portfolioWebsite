ABOUT ME

My name is Andrea Trigg and I am a web designer/developer based out of Indianapolis, IN. I currently attend Indiana Univeristy-Purdue University Indianapolis, where I am studying Media Arts and Science with a concentration in web development. Alongside my classwork, I have had the pleasure of being the assistant webmaster for IUPUI’s Center for Teaching and Learning for the 2018-2019 school year, as well as the webmaster for Women In Technology for the 2019-2020 school year. As far as teaching experience goes, I’ve worked under Travis Faas as a Teacher’s Assistant for NEWM N220, and I have gained experience with high school students working with the School of Informatics and Computing’s iDEW program. I am pursuing an accelerated master’s degree in Media Arts and Science and dream of becoming a college professor upon graduation.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my fellow sisters of Alpha Sigma Alpha as well as family and friends, writing, drawing on my iPad and taking many, many photos of my beloved cat, Daphne. One of my favorite hobbies besides coding is music; from listening to it to singing to producing music on Logic Pro X, music is a big part of my life. I currently am learning Sonic Pi outside of my coursework so I can further expand my knowledge on music and what goes into making it.
